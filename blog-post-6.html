Beginner's Guide to Meta Business Manager

Meta Business Manager is the foundation of any serious Facebook advertising strategy. It's where you manage your Facebook Page, Instagram account, ad campaigns, Pixel, and team members all in one place.

If you're ready to run Facebook ads professionally, you need to set up Business Manager. This step-by-step guide will walk you through everything.

What You'll Learn

  • How to create a Meta Business Manager account
  • How to install the Facebook Pixel
  • How to verify your domain
  • How to connect Instagram
  • How to add a Page and create a campaign
💡 Important: Meta Business Manager is free. You do not need to spend any money to set it up.

What is Meta Business Manager?

Meta Business Manager (formerly Facebook Business Manager) is a free tool that helps you:

  • Manage assets: Pages, ad accounts, Instagram accounts, and Pixels
  • Control access: Give team members specific permissions without sharing login details
  • Run ads: Create and manage campaigns at scale
  • Track performance: Monitor analytics and ROI
🎯 Think of it as: The control center for all your Meta (Facebook/Instagram) business activities.

Step 1: Create Your Meta Business Manager Account

1

Create Business Manager

Follow these steps:

  1. Go to business.facebook.com
  2. Click "Create Account"
  3. Enter your business name and your name
  4. Enter your business email
  5. Click "Submit"

Pro tip: Use your business email, not your personal Facebook email.

Step 2: Add Your Facebook Page

2

Add Your Page

Once your account is created:

  1. In Business Manager, go to "Accounts" → "Pages"
  2. Click "Add"
  3. Select "Add a Page"
  4. Search for your Facebook Page
  5. Click "Add Page"

Pro tip: You can add multiple Pages if you have more than one business.

Step 3: Connect Your Instagram Account

3

Connect Instagram

To run Instagram ads, you need to connect your account:

  1. In Business Manager, go to "Accounts" → "Instagram Accounts"
  2. Click "Add"
  3. Select "Add an Instagram Account"
  4. Enter your Instagram login details
  5. Click "Connect"

Pro tip: Make sure your Instagram account is set to "Business" or "Creator" mode.

💡 Pro Tip: You can also connect Instagram by linking it through your Facebook Page settings.

Step 4: Create Your Ad Account

4

Create Ad Account

You need an ad account to run campaigns:

  1. In Business Manager, go to "Accounts" → "Ad Accounts"
  2. Click "Add"
  3. Select "Create a New Ad Account"
  4. Fill in your business details
  5. Set your currency and timezone
  6. Click "Create"

Pro tip: Your timezone and currency cannot be changed later—choose carefully.

Step 5: Install the Facebook Pixel

5

Install Pixel

The Pixel tracks website visitors and conversions:

  1. In Business Manager, go to "Data Sources" → "Pixels"
  2. Click "Add" → "Create a New Pixel"
  3. Name your Pixel (use your business name)
  4. Click "Create"
  5. Choose how to install:
    • Install manually: Copy the code and paste it into your website's header
    • Use a partner: Shopify, WordPress, or other platform integration
    • Email instructions: Send the code to a developer

Pro tip: The Pixel is the most important tracking tool you'll use. Install it before running any ads.

⚠️ Critical: Without the Pixel, your ads will be 50-80% less effective. Install it immediately.

Step 6: Verify Your Domain

6

Verify Domain

Domain verification gives you more control over your website links:

  1. In Business Manager, go to "Brand Safety" → "Domains"
  2. Click "Add"
  3. Enter your website domain (e.g., yourwebsite.com)
  4. Choose a verification method:
    • Meta-tag verification: Add a code to your website's header
    • DNS verification: Add a TXT record to your domain
    • File upload: Upload an HTML file to your website
  5. Click "Verify"

Pro tip: Meta-tag verification is the easiest method for most people.

Step 7: Add Team Members

7

Add Team Members

If you work with others, you can add them to Business Manager:

  1. In Business Manager, go to "Users" → "People"
  2. Click "Add"
  3. Enter the person's email
  4. Choose their role:
    • Admin: Full control
    • Employee: Limited access
  5. Assign permissions to specific assets (Pages, ad accounts, etc.)
  6. Click "Send Request"

Pro tip: Never share your personal login. Use Business Manager to give team members controlled access.

💡 Best Practice: Always use Business Manager to manage team access. It's more secure and gives you full control.

Step 8: Create Your First Campaign

8

Create Campaign

Now you're ready to run your first ad:

  1. In Business Manager, go to "Ads Manager"
  2. Click "Create"
  3. Choose your campaign objective (Awareness, Traffic, Engagement, Leads, Sales)
  4. Set your budget and schedule
  5. Choose your target audience
  6. Create your ad creative (image, video, text, headline, CTA)
  7. Review and click "Publish"

Pro tip: Start with a small budget ($5-$20/day) to test what works before scaling up.

🚀 Ready to Launch? Your Meta Business Manager is now fully set up. Start with a small test campaign to learn the platform.

Final Thoughts

Setting up Meta Business Manager is one of the best investments of time you'll make for your business. It gives you full control over your Facebook and Instagram advertising, with all the tools you need to track, optimize, and scale your campaigns.

The setup process takes 30-60 minutes, but it's well worth it. Once you're set up, you'll have a professional advertising infrastructure that can grow with your business.

🎯 Key Takeaway: Meta Business Manager is free, essential, and the foundation of professional Facebook advertising. Set it up today.
