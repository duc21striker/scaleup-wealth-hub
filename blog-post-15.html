Best Free Email Marketing Tools

You don't need to spend money to start email marketing. Here are the best free tools to build your email list and run campaigns.

What You'll Learn

  • The best free email marketing tools
  • Features and limitations of each
  • Which tool is right for you
💡 Pro Tip: Start with a free plan. You can always upgrade later as your list grows.

1. Mailchimp

Mailchimp

Free plan: 500 contacts, 1,000 emails/month. Popular and easy to use with great templates.

2. Brevo (formerly Sendinblue)

Brevo

Free plan: Unlimited contacts, 300 emails/day. Great for automation and SMS marketing.

3. MailerLite

MailerLite

Free plan: 1,000 subscribers, 12,000 emails/month. Clean interface and powerful features.

4. Beehiiv

Beehiiv

Free plan: Unlimited subscribers, 1,000 emails/month. Perfect for newsletter-focused creators.

5. n8n (Automation)

n8n

Free self-hosted version. Automate email workflows, lead capture, and integration with other tools.

Comparison Table

Tool Free Contacts Free Emails Best For
Mailchimp 500 1,000/mo Beginners
Brevo Unlimited 300/day Automation
MailerLite 1,000 12,000/mo Design & Ease
Beehiiv Unlimited 1,000/mo Newsletters

Which Tool Should You Choose?

  • Mailchimp: Best for beginners with a small list
  • Brevo: Best for unlimited contacts and automation
  • MailerLite: Best for design and user experience
  • Beehiiv: Best for newsletter creators
  • n8n: Best for custom automation workflows
🎯 Pro Tip: Start with MailerLite or Brevo. Both have generous free plans and are easy to use.

Final Thoughts

You don't need expensive software to start email marketing. All these tools have free plans that are powerful enough to grow your business.

🚀 Ready to Start? Pick one tool, create a free account, and start building your email list today!
