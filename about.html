Our Story

From Vision to Reality

ScaleUp Wealth Hub was born from a simple observation: many people want to earn online but don't know where to start. With the rise of AI and digital tools, the gap between those who know how to use technology and those who don't is growing wider.

We started this platform to bridge that gap. What began as a small blog sharing AI tools has grown into a comprehensive learning hub with students from across the world.

2024 Grew to 500+ students
2025 Expanded to full platform
