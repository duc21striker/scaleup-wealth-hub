Build an Email List from Scratch

Your email list is your most valuable digital asset. It's the only audience you truly own. Here's how to build one from scratch.

What You'll Learn

  • How to create a lead magnet
  • How to build a landing page
  • How to set up forms and automation
💡 The Truth: The best time to start building your email list was yesterday. The second best time is today.

Step 1: Create a Lead Magnet

1

Lead Magnet

A lead magnet is something valuable you give away in exchange for an email address.

Examples:

  • Free guide or eBook
  • Checklist or template
  • Video training
  • Free consultation
  • Discount or coupon
  • Tool or resource list

Pro tip: Your lead magnet should solve a specific problem for your audience.

Step 2: Build a Landing Page

2

Landing Page

A landing page is a simple page designed to capture emails. It should have:

  • A clear headline
  • Explanation of the lead magnet's value
  • A simple signup form
  • A call to action button
  • Minimal distractions

Tools: Use free tools like Carrd, MailerLite, or ConvertKit.

🎯 Quick Tip: A landing page should have one goal—get the email address. No menus, no links, just one button.

Step 3: Set Up Signup Forms

3

Signup Forms

Place signup forms on your website strategically:

  • Header: Top of every page
  • Sidebar: On blog posts
  • Footer: Bottom of every page
  • Pop-up: Exit-intent or timed
  • Inline: Within blog content

Step 4: Add Automation

4

Automation

Set up automated email sequences:

  • Welcome email: Thank them and deliver the lead magnet
  • Nurture sequence: Build trust over 3-5 emails
  • Re-engagement: Bring back inactive subscribers

Pro tip: Your welcome email is the most important. Make it personal and valuable.

Step 5: Drive Traffic

5

Drive Traffic

Get people to your landing page:

  • Social media: Share your lead magnet
  • Blog posts: Promote within content
  • YouTube: Mention in videos
  • Guest posts: Write for other sites
  • Paid ads: Run targeted campaigns

Quick Checklist

  • ✅ Create a valuable lead magnet
  • ✅ Build a simple landing page
  • ✅ Set up signup forms
  • ✅ Create automated welcome emails
  • ✅ Drive traffic to your landing page

Final Thoughts

Building an email list takes time, but it's one of the best investments you can make. Every email you collect is a direct relationship with a potential customer.

🚀 Ready to Start? Create a simple lead magnet today. Don't overthink it—start with something small and improve later.
