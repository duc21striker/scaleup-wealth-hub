My Digital Marketing Workflow

How do I deliver results for clients consistently? It comes down to a structured workflow that leverages AI, automation, and best practices.

Here's a behind-the-scenes look at my digital marketing workflow from client inquiry to delivery.

What You'll Learn

  • My complete client process
  • How I use AI and automation
  • How I deliver consistent results
💡 Pro Tip: A structured workflow saves time and ensures consistent quality for every client.

Step 1: Client Inquiry & Discovery

1

Discovery

I start by understanding the client's business, goals, and challenges. I ask:

  • What are your business goals?
  • Who is your target audience?
  • What have you tried before?
  • What's your budget and timeline?

Step 2: Strategy Development

2

Strategy

I develop a customized strategy based on the client's needs. This includes:

  • Website audit and recommendations
  • SEO strategy
  • Facebook Ads plan
  • Content marketing calendar
  • Email marketing strategy

Step 3: Setup & Implementation

3

Implementation

I set up all the systems and tools needed:

  • Website and landing pages
  • Google Analytics and Search Console
  • Facebook Pixel and Meta Business Manager
  • Email marketing tools
  • Automation workflows

Step 4: Content Creation

4

Content Creation

I create content that drives results:

  • Blog posts and articles
  • Social media content
  • Video and graphics
  • Email newsletters
  • Ad copy and creative
🎯 Pro Tip: I use AI tools like ChatGPT and Canva AI to speed up content creation without sacrificing quality.

Step 5: Launch & Execution

5

Launch

I execute the strategy and launch campaigns:

  • Publish website content
  • Launch Facebook Ads
  • Send email campaigns
  • Post on social media
  • Monitor performance

Step 6: Monitoring & Optimization

6

Optimize

I continuously monitor and improve performance:

  • Track analytics and KPIs
  • Adjust ad campaigns
  • Improve SEO
  • Test and refine content
  • Report results to clients

Step 7: Delivery & Reporting

7

Delivery

I provide clear, transparent reporting:

  • Weekly or monthly reports
  • Performance metrics and insights
  • Recommendations for improvement
  • Next steps and strategy updates

Tools I Use

  • AI: ChatGPT, Claude, Canva AI
  • Website: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, GitHub Pages
  • Analytics: Google Analytics, Google Search Console
  • Ads: Meta Business Manager, Facebook Ads
  • Email: MailerLite, Brevo
  • Design: Canva, Figma
  • Automation: n8n

Final Thoughts

A structured workflow ensures every client gets consistent, high-quality results. Whether you're a business owner or a freelancer, having a clear process helps you deliver better results and grow your business.

🚀 Ready to Build Your Workflow? Start by documenting your current process. Identify areas where you can improve and systems you can automate.
